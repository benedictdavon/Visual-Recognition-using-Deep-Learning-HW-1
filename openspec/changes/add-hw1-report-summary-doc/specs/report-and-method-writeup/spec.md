## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Report-prep docs SHALL enforce rubric-aligned structure
Documentation used to draft the final homework report SHALL explicitly map to rubric-required sections and SHALL remain consistent with the report policy in course instructions.

#### Scenario: Rubric alignment is audit-ready
- **WHEN** the report-prep document is reviewed before PDF submission
- **THEN** rubric-required sections and constraints are clearly listed and can be checked without opening external notes

### Requirement: Additional-experiment claims SHALL include interpretation
Any additional experiment documented for report preparation SHALL include hypothesis, expected mechanism, observed result, and implication, and SHALL NOT be presented as satisfied by hyperparameter tuning alone.

#### Scenario: Additional experiment meets policy intent
- **WHEN** the evaluator inspects an "additional experiment" claim
- **THEN** the claim includes interpretation and implication beyond simple hyperparameter-change reporting
